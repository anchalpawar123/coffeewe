Our Story

Obsessed With
The Perfect Cup

BrewNoir began in 2012 as a single roasting lab in Mumbai. What started as a weekend obsession became India's most celebrated specialty coffee house — with 8 locations, a micro-roastery, and a global sourcing network.

We believe every cup of coffee has a terroir — a story of its soil, its farmers, its journey. We just make sure that story ends perfectly in your hands.
